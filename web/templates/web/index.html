Inside to Outside

You may not be able to use foreign services such as YouTube, Twitter, SoundCloud, and others. YouBute is located in the USA which means that YouBute can provide services for you. It stores media in its host for you.

First, you make a connection with the YouBute server, then you want it to store videos for you and it does. YouBute uses pytube library to store media from the YouTube.

How You Access Media?

First of all, you request to the server, then server wants pytube to download the given URL which refers to the YouTube video. Then, the video gets ready for the YouBute host and you have access to your media.

Now, you have 6 hours to download and share the given URL.
